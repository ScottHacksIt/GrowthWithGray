About Gray

My Story

I came to this work through my own journey of healing. Like so many of us, I spent years disconnected from my body — living from the neck up, pushing through, and wondering why something still felt missing.

It was through somatic work that everything shifted. I discovered that the body holds wisdom, memory, and an incredible capacity for healing that talk therapy alone couldn't reach. That experience changed the trajectory of my life and my career.

Today, I bring that same integration to my clients — weaving together relational therapy, body-based practices, and deep compassion to support people in becoming more fully themselves.

My Approach

My work is grounded in four core principles that guide every session.

Body-Centered

The body is not separate from the mind. I help you tune into the wisdom your body already holds.

Trauma-Informed

Safety comes first. Every session respects your pace, your boundaries, and your nervous system.

Relational

Healing happens in connection. The therapeutic relationship itself is a space for growth.

Holistic

I see you as a whole person — mind, body, heart, and spirit — and honor all parts of your experience.

Beyond the Office

When I'm not in session, you'll find me moving my own body — whether that's through dance, hiking, or simply slowing down enough to feel the ground beneath me.

I believe deeply in practicing what I teach: presence, embodiment, and the courage to keep growing. This work isn't just my profession — it's my way of life.
