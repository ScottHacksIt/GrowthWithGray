LMFT Therapy

As a licensed Marriage and Family Therapist, I specialize in helping individuals and couples navigate the complexities of their inner lives and relationships.

Together, we'll explore the patterns, beliefs, and relational dynamics that may be keeping you stuck — and find new ways to move through the world with greater ease and authenticity.

Common Areas of Focus

  • Anxiety and depression
  • Relationship challenges
  • Communication and conflict
  • Attachment and intimacy
  • Life transitions and identity
  • Trauma and PTSD

Somatic Sex Therapy

Somatic sex therapy brings the body into the therapeutic process. Rather than just talking about your experiences, we work with sensation, breath, movement, and embodied awareness to access deeper layers of healing.

This modality is especially powerful for people navigating sexual concerns, body image challenges, disconnection from pleasure, or the lingering effects of trauma on intimacy.

What to Expect

Sessions are always boundaried, consent-driven, and guided by your comfort level. Somatic sex therapy is a professional, therapeutic practice — distinct from sexual surrogacy. We work at the pace that feels right for you.

Who This Is For

  • Individuals exploring their relationship with their body and sexuality
  • Couples seeking deeper physical and emotional intimacy
  • Survivors of sexual trauma seeking body-based healing
  • Anyone experiencing sexual pain, difficulty with arousal, or disconnection

Coaching

Coaching is different from therapy. While therapy often explores the past to heal the present, coaching starts with where you are now and helps you move toward where you want to be.

I work with clients who are ready for action — whether that means building better relationship skills, deepening self-awareness, or creating a more embodied, fulfilling life.

Coaching Can Help With

  • Personal growth and self-discovery
  • Relationship skills and communication
  • Building confidence and embodied presence
  • Navigating dating and new relationships
  • Sexual wellness and education
