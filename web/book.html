Growing into Your Pleasure

A Compassionate Guided Journal for Women Healing from Trauma, Reclaiming Safety, and Rediscovering Joy in Sexuality

This guided journal is a gentle, compassionate companion for women who are ready to reconnect with their bodies, reclaim a sense of safety, and rediscover joy in their sexuality after trauma.

Written by Gray Moulton from years of experience as an LMFT and Somatic Sex Therapist, this journal offers thoughtful prompts, somatic exercises, and reflective practices designed to meet you exactly where you are — with no pressure, no timeline, and no judgment.

Whether you're just beginning your healing journey or deepening work you've already started, this journal provides a safe, private space to explore at your own pace.
